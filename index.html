Rocketsheet: the free spreadsheet to record and analyze launch data for competitions or fun!

  • Created specifically for neat & efficient data keeping.
  • You can customize the data entries to work the way you need it to.
  • Perfect for model rocket contests!
  • Free, Rocketsheet is based on Google Sheets , only a free Google account is needed.
  • Works on phones, tablets, & laptops so every team member has access to the spreadsheet, whether you are out on the field or at a desk.
